PRIVACY POLICY

NOVA STORM BY JWORKS

Data Collection

Nova Storm does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The game runs entirely on your device. No user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no network requests are made during gameplay.

Local Storage

Game progress (high scores, unlocked ships, weapon upgrades, settings preferences) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone else.

Permissions

The app may request vibration access (for haptic feedback) and audio playback. No camera, microphone, contacts, location, or other sensitive permissions are required or requested.

Children

Nova Storm does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because no personal data is collected from anyone, the game is safe for all ages.
